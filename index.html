Java Steganography Encoding Program

Fall 2016

Steganography is the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file, message, image, or video. Whereas cryptography is the practice of protecting the contents of a message alone, steganography is concerned with concealing the fact that a secret message is being sent as well as concealing the contents of the message.

My program takes normal images (png, jpg, etc) and encodes a secret message in them. You can also use it to decode messages out of images that have been encoded by my program. The program is written in Java.
